# Content Ops Weekly (Queer Atlas)

This keeps the product alive, trustworthy, and rank-worthy.

## Weekly cadence (60-90 min)

1. World news refresh
- Add 3-5 real updates in `/now` (rights/safety/openings/major events)
- Remove stale or duplicated items

2. Event hygiene
- Review upcoming events and remove dead links
- Verify date accuracy
- Mark stale quality as "needs refresh" where necessary

3. City quality sprint
- Pick 3 cities/week
- Improve at least:
  - 2 venue descriptions per city
  - quick guide details (practical tips, vibe cues)
  - one transport/safety/local-tip enhancement

4. Safety moderation review
- Review reports queue
- Resolve obvious spam/abuse first
- Keep moderation notes short and consistent

## Monthly cadence

1. Dead-link cleanup pass across top traffic cities
2. Re-rank Top 15 destinations with editorial rationale
3. Audit "needs refresh" queue and clear oldest items first

## Quality bar checklist (for any new content)

- Unique (not copied text)
- Accurate (date/location/link)
- Useful (practical for real users)
- Tone-aligned (cool, queer, confident, inclusive)
- Safe (no risky or misleading claims)
